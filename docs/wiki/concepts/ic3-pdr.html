IC3 / PDR

Safety-checking algorithms that construct inductive invariants (certificates).

DRAFT: Wiki page.
Wiki Model checking Certificate DS-012

Idea

IC3/PDR builds a sequence of over-approximating “frames” of reachable states and derives inductive clauses until it:

  • finds a real trace to an unsafe state (DISPROVED), or
  • finds an inductive invariant (PROVED).

Why it is attractive in UBHNL

  • The positive result is an invariant, which is easy to validate via SAT/SMT obligations (checkable certificate).
  • It can be the primary backend for Frag_TS, with fallback to BMC when needed.

Consequences

  • Integration requires a stable format for Init/Trans/Safe and for invariants.
  • The planner must manage budgets and fallbacks (IC3 does not guarantee fast success on all instances).
