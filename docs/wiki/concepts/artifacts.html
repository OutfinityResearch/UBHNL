Artifacts (exchanged/verified objects)

What the orchestrator/backends produce: lemmas, cores, invariants, strategies, KC objects, etc.

DRAFT: Wiki page.
Wiki Orchestrator Certificate Origin DS-012

Why “artifacts” exist

  • Backends do not return only a status; they return objects that enable refinement, caching, and explanations.
  • The orchestrator routes them between tasks and validates them (when applicable).

Consequences

  • All artifacts must be serializable and linked to origins (for explanation and replay).
  • Some artifacts are certificates (invariant/strategy), others are only support (unsat core).
