MaxSAT / PBO / OMT (logical optimization)

Optimization over Boolean/SMT constraints: find the “best” satisfying solution.

DRAFT: Wiki page.
Wiki Certificate MIP DS-011

Definitions (simplified)

  • MaxSAT: maximize the number of satisfied clauses (or minimize the cost of unsatisfied clauses).
  • PBO: pseudo-Boolean optimization (linear constraints over Booleans).
  • OMT: optimization modulo theories (SMT plus an objective).

Certificates

For OPTIMAL, UBHNL requires:

  • a witness assignment (the solution), and
  • evidence that no better solution exists (typically: UNSAT for a stricter bound, with a checkable certificate).

In UBHNL

  • Modeled as Frag_Opt; it can be reduced to SAT (bound tightening) or solved natively (MaxSAT/OMT solvers).
  • The planner uses a tactic: “solve with bound k → improve → when it becomes UNSAT, you have optimality”.

Relevant specs
