SDP / SOS (Semidefinite programming, Sum-of-Squares)

Methods for semidefinite constraints and algebraic certificates (SOS) for polynomial inequalities.

DRAFT: Wiki page.
Wiki Certificate CAD/QE DS-010

Definition

  • SDP: optimization with constraints of the form “a matrix is positive semidefinite”.
  • SOS: prove non-negativity of a polynomial by expressing it as a sum of squares.

Certificates

  • dual feasible solution (bounds/infeasibility),
  • SOS decomposition (checkable if rationalized/normalized).

In UBHNL

  • Treated as a numeric fragment (Frag_SDP_SOS) with external/native backends.
  • UBHNL does not lower SDP into UBH by default; it checks certificates in the trusted core.

Relevant specs

  • DS-010 (Frag_SDP_SOS)
  • DS-011 (certificate SDP/SOS)
