MIP (Mixed Integer Programming)

Optimization/feasibility over linear constraints with integrality; primal/dual certificates.

DRAFT: Wiki page.
Wiki Certificate Optimizare DS-011

Definition

MIP solves: 

min/max  c·x
subject to  A x ≤ b
            x_i ∈ ℤ for some i

Certificates

  • feasible: a checkable primal solution.
  • infeasible: a dual certificate / Farkas-style proof (dual ray).
  • optimal: primal solution plus a matching dual bound certificate.

In UBHNL

  • Treated as a numeric fragment (Frag_MIP) that typically remains native; UBH is used as an anchor for checking, not as a numeric solver.
  • Results are accepted only with checkable dual certificates.
