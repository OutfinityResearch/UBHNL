Definition

A factor graph (or tensor network) represents a global function as a product of local factors. Belief propagation is an approximate message-passing algorithm for marginals/conditionals.

In UBHNL

  • Exact: prefer reduction to WMC/KC (checkable).
  • Approximate: BP/variational methods; results are probabilistic and require audit metadata.
