ATP by saturation (resolution / superposition)

Automated theorem proving for FOL (+ equality): proofs as checkable traces.

DRAFT: Wiki page.
Wiki Certificate Rewriting DS-011

Definition

Saturation-based ATP tries to derive a contradiction or construct a proof by repeatedly applying inference rules (resolution, and superposition for equality) until saturation or until the empty clause is derived.

In UBHNL

  • Modeled as a backend for Frag_FOL_Eq.
  • Can produce lemmas/proof traces that are verified by a small checker (trusted core).

Certificate

The typical certificate is a proof trace (resolution/superposition steps) that can be checked deterministically.
