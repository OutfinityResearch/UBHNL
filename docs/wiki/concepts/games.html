Game solving (reachability / parity games)

A verification framing as a game: a winning strategy is the certificate.

DRAFT: Wiki page.
Wiki Model checking Certificate DS-011

Definition

Some verification problems (especially liveness/fixpoint properties) can be reduced to graph games. A solution is a strategy for one of the players.

Certificate

  • A winning strategy (checkable by simulation/obligations).
  • In some cases, strategy certificates can be converted into invariants.

In UBHNL

  • Modeled as Frag_Game and integrated as a backend for verification (especially for TS/automata workflows).
  • The orchestrator can choose game solving or IC3/BMC depending on the property and budget.

Relevant specs

  • DS-011 (strategy as certificate)
