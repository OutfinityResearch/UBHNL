DRAT / LRAT (SAT UNSAT certificates)

Certificate formats for SAT UNSAT; checked deterministically in the trusted core.

DRAFT: Wiki page.
Wiki Certificate SAT DS-011

Idea

For a CNF formula, a solver can produce a proof trace that demonstrates UNSAT. A checker validates each step and confirms the final contradiction.

Why they appear in UBHNL

  • They are a practical standard anchor for accepting UNSAT in Boolean reasoning.
  • They allow SAT backends to be untrusted yet verifiable.
  • UBHNL can lower many claims to CNF UNSAT, and accepts only with DRAT/LRAT evidence.
