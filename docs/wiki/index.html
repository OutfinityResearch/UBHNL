Core math

Bit / Boolean

{0,1}, truth/falsehood, and the base evaluation model.

GF(2)

The field with 2 elements: XOR is +, AND is ·.

ANF (Algebraic Normal Form)

A canonical polynomial form of Boolean functions over GF(2).

UBH kernel

UBH (Universal Boolean Hypergraph)

Minimal IR: XOR/AND/CONST/VAR plus assertions.

Wire / NodeId

The identity of a wire/node in the hash-consed DAG.

Hash-consing

Structural sharing by deduplicating identical subgraphs.

Local simplifications

O(1) rewrites that preserve satisfiability.

Design decisions

These pages explain “why” (trade-offs) and the consequences for the architecture/specs.

Minimal kernel + trusted core

Strict separation: solving vs checking.

Strict vocabulary policy

Typos do not become new symbols; reproducibility.

DSL: “single @ per line”

Clarity and ambiguity avoidance.

SAT + native XOR

Avoid XOR blow-up when encoding to CNF.

Mandatory certificates

UNSAT/OPTIMAL without evidence ⇒ UNKNOWN.

Schema engine (CEGAR)

Compression: keep rules, avoid full grounding.

Fragments + orchestrator

Not every problem is “just SAT”.

UNKNOWN policy

Preferable to any unverifiable claim.
