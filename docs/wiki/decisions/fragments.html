Decision: fragments + orchestrator (not “everything is SAT”)

Separates “what problem is this?” from “how do we solve it?”, with certificate contracts per method.

Wiki Fragment Orchestrator DS-010

The decision

UBHNL does not assume that all problems should be immediately reduced to UBH/SAT. Instead, the system classifies the problem (fragment) and selects appropriate backends/tactics.

Why

  • Some domains have specialized backends (MIP/SDP/CAD) with better certificates than a brute-force Boolean reduction.
  • Some properties (TS safety) are solved more naturally via invariants (IC3/PDR).

Consequences

  • Encodings/lowerings and acceptance criteria must be defined per fragment.
  • The planner becomes a first-class component, with policies/budgets/logs.
