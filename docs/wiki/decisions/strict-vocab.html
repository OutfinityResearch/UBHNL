Decision: strict vocabulary (unknown symbol = load error)

Prevents “typo turns into new symbol” and makes theory files reproducible and auditable.

DRAFT: decision page.
Wiki Lexicon DSL DS-008

The decision

In CNL/DSL, any bare identifier must exist in the vocabulary (lexicon + exports). If it does not exist: load error.

Why

  • Reproducibility: the same theory loaded today and tomorrow produces the same IR.
  • Safety: a typo does not silently become a new symbol.
  • Engineer-friendly UX: errors are local (line/column) and immediately fixable.

Consequences

  • You need a good lexicon (domains/constants/predicates) and an export mechanism (@name:Type).
  • The parser must clearly distinguish references ($x) from vocabulary symbols (name).
