Design Choices

Short rationale for the main architectural decisions.

Kernel IR

XOR/AND/CONST gates over GF(2) give a minimal, universal Boolean basis with a direct polynomial interpretation and efficient structural hashing.

Solver Strategy

A SAT backbone with native XOR reasoning avoids CNF blowups and matches the circuit structure typical of UBH workloads.

Schema Engine

Quantifiers are handled with lazy instantiation to avoid full grounding and keep large theories compact.

CNL Front-end

Controlled natural language trades expressivity for deterministic parsing and predictable compilation to the kernel.
